What to realistically expect from SEO

SEO isn't instant — but it's the only marketing channel that gets cheaper and more effective over time. Here's an honest timeline.

Weeks 1–4
Foundation & Indexing
Your site gets submitted to Google, sitemap and robots.txt are configured, and Google begins crawling. You show up for your exact business name.
Months 2–3
Local Visibility Builds
Your Google Business Profile and local schema start placing you in "near me" searches. You begin appearing in map results for your city.
Months 3–6
Keyword Rankings Take Hold
Service-specific pages start ranking for your target keywords. Organic traffic begins — slowly at first, then noticeably. Competitors that outrank you become identifiable.
Months 6–12
Traffic Compounds
Rankings solidify, click-through rates improve, and your site earns authority. New leads arrive consistently — without any ad spend. Most clients see 2–4× more inbound inquiries.
Year 1+
Established Authority
You dominate your local market for high-intent searches. Competitors are chasing you. Your site is a lead generation asset — not just a business card.
Unlike ads, SEO compounds. Every month your site is optimized, it gets harder for competitors to displace you — and your cost-per-lead keeps dropping.

FAQ

Questions? Answered.

What if I don't have a domain or hosting yet? +
No problem. I'll walk you through getting a domain and set up hosting for you. Most of my clients use Netlify (free for static sites) or Namecheap for domains. I handle all of it.
How long does a website take to build? +
Typically about 1 week depending on the package. Some simpler builds move faster when you have your content ready upfront.
Do you offer payment plans? +
Yes. For any project over $499, you can split 50/50 (half now, half at launch) or into 3 monthly payments. Just mention it when you reach out and we'll figure out what works.
Will my site show up on Google? +
Every site I build includes sitemap.xml, robots.txt, and Google Search Console setup — so Google can find and index your site the moment it goes live. Growth and Premium packages also include active keyword optimization.
Can I update the site myself after it's built? +
Absolutely. I can build on a platform you can manage yourself (like Squarespace or WordPress), or I can handle all updates on a monthly retainer. Your call.
What industries do you work with? +
I work with any local service business — HVAC, insurance, real estate, contractors, restaurants, medical practices, you name it. If you serve customers in a local area, I can build a site that gets you found.
