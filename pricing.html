Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

What determines where in the price range I land? +
Scope and complexity. The more pages, custom features, integrations, or content you need, the higher the investment. Simple sites with clear goals land at the lower end; sites requiring custom functionality or large page counts land higher. I'll give you a specific quote after a free discovery call.
Do I own the website after it's built? +
Your domain is always yours — you own and register it, full stop. With the one-time build packages, you also own the code and content outright. With Website as a Service, we build and host the site on your behalf; the site stays live as long as your subscription is active. If you cancel after the 12-month minimum, the site comes down — but your domain is yours to take anywhere.
How long does a website build take? +
Most builds are delivered in about 1 week. Simpler projects can move faster when your content is ready upfront. I'll give you a clear timeline before we start.
What do I need to provide? +
Your logo (or we can create a simple wordmark), photos if you have them, and the key information about your business — services, location, contact details. I handle all the design, writing, and technical setup. The less you have, the more I can help fill in.
Can I upgrade my package later? +
Absolutely. Many clients start with an Essential site and expand as their business grows. I build with clean, scalable code so adding pages or features later is straightforward.
Do you offer payment plans? +
Yes. For one-time builds I typically structure a 50% deposit upfront and 50% on delivery. For clients who prefer no large upfront cost, the Website as a Service plan is the cleanest solution — one flat monthly rate, 12-month minimum, and we handle everything. Maintenance plans (Starter through Authority) are separate ongoing services available after launch.
